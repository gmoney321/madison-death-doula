What is a Death Doula?

A death doula is a person who assists in the dying process, much like a midwife or doula does with the birthing process

  • Create death plans
  • Provide spiritual, psychological, and social support
  • Funeral and memorial service planning
  • Guiding mourners in their rights and responsibilities
